About Us

Welcome to the Itsa Committee! We are a dynamic tech committee dedicated to bridging the gap between technology and creativity. Our mission is to provide students with invaluable knowledge and hands-on experience through a diverse range of tech events and seminars featuring industry experts.

Our vibrant team of tech enthusiasts is committed to merging technology with creativity, making learning both engaging and enjoyable. From insightful seminars to innovative tech games, we strive to create experiences that not only educate but also inspire.
